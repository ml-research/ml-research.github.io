Project Seminar

Practical Research in AI (PRAI)

Work in small teams on open AIML Lab research questions, from trustworthy AI to generative modelling and neuro-symbolic reasoning.

Back to teaching overview Project workspace

Programme overview

PRAI offers hands-on experience in designing experiments, developing prototypes, and presenting research findings. Teams work closely with AIML supervisors on current challenges.

  • • Responsible AI and assurance pipelines.
  • • Generative modelling and diffusion safety.
  • • Neuro-symbolic reasoning and explainability.
  • • Applied AI in healthcare, mobility, and sustainability.
