Research Topics

Our work spans statistical relational AI, generative modeling, and trustworthy ML systems in close collaboration with cognitive science, robotics, and domain experts.

People

We are computer scientists, cognitive scientists, and engineers working across TU Darmstadt, Hessian.AI, and international partners. Meet the team that drives our research agenda.

Teaching

We offer core courses in machine learning and artificial intelligence, hands-on seminars, and supervision for bachelor and master theses. Thesis supervision is available for TU Darmstadt students only.
