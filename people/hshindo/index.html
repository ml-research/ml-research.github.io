Mission. My research interest is mainly around developing machine learning algorithms incorporating symbolic (logic, discrete structures, etc.) and numerical (probability, optimization, etc.) computations. Currently, I am mainly working on the Neuro-Symbolic approach, where the symbolic logic and neural-based models are incorporated to solve challenging problems requiring the models to perform perception from raw input and reasoning on high-level concepts.
