Mission. My research interests are centered around new deep models for tractable probabilistic inference. In helping computers build models of the world and do statistics efficiently and accurately so that they can help humans make better predictions, discover patterns and understand data more easily.

Bio. I'm a PhD student of Machine Learning at the Computer Science Department of the TU Darmstadt University, Germany. After receiving my M.Sc. from the University of Freiburg in 2012, I joined the Machine Learning group in TU Dortmund and then moved to TU Darmstadt University.

Timeline.
