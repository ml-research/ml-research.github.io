Mission. My research focuses on deep learning, symbolic AI systems, and their combinations. In particular, I am interested in Neuro-Symbolic AI systems combining deep representation learning with symbolic computations to improve their ability to perform relational and analogical reasoning. Currently, I am developing large scale visual datasets, where class labels are derived from rule-based logic.
