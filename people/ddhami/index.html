Mission. My research interest is multi-faceted and is currently centered around neuro-symbolic AI, probabilistic models (incorporating relational information and causality) and graph neural networks.

Bio. I'm a PostDoc in the Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Lab at the Computer Science Department of the TU Darmstadt University, Germany. I received my master's fron Indiana University Bloomington and my PhD from University of Texas at Dallas.

Timeline.
